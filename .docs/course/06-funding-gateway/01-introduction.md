# Funding Your Gateway Wallet

Before making gasless payments, you need to fund your wallet with testnet USDC and deposit it into Circle Gateway.

## What You'll Do

1. **Request** testnet USDC from the Circle faucet
2. **Verify** your on-chain balance using viem
3. **Deposit** USDC into the Gateway Wallet contract
4. **Confirm** your Gateway balance

After the deposit, all payments are gasless — you only need one on-chain transaction to start.
